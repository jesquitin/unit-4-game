How to play the game

  1. You will be given a random number at the start of the game.
  2. There are four crystals below. Each crystal will be assigned point value. By clicking on a crytal yo will add a specific amount of points to your total
  3. Your will win by matching your total to the random number or lose if your total goes abover the random number
  4. The value of each crystal is hidden until it is clicked
  5. Each time the game start a different point value will be given to the crystals
